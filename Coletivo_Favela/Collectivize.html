Collectivity in your community is an essential component in combating gentrification, eviction and unwanted development. Collectivity, however, takes work and time on behalf of many community members. One way to combat speculation in your community, along with collectivity and unity, is collective titling.

Collective title has the potential to provide quality affordable housing through shared equity and institutional arrangements that directly and indirectly provide access to economic, social and cultural capital in your community.

Existing Community Land Trust Models

Did you know that Afro-Brazilian Quilombos function like a Community Land Trust? Community Land Trusts are increasing in popularity and are popping up all over the world in areas where evictions, displacement and gentrification are taking place. Take a look at some existing CLT models.
