Regularization is an important step in gaining a right to the city by securing land tenure to prevent eviction and displacement. Regularization can be done collectively, to prevent community-wide displacement, or individually. While it is almost always recommended to gain collective title over individual title, whether you decide to regularize collectively or individually is up to you and your community.

If you are to regularize individually, it is important to be aware of the implications that follow reguarization and how to prevent gentrification in your neighborhood.
